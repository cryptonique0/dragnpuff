# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is House of the DragNs?
House of the DragNs is a decentralized social game on Base that combines NFTs, tokens, and Farcaster social engagement. Players mint DragN'Puff NFTs, choose a House, and compete through social actions.

### What is a DragN'Puff NFT?
A unique ERC721 NFT from the generative art collection by @nomadicframe. Each NFT has 7 trait categories with 111 unique traits across 7 houses.

### What is the $NOM token?
$NOM is an ERC20 token used for:
- Governance voting
- Reduced minting fees for large holders
- In-game rewards and incentives

## Houses & Gameplay

### What are the 7 Houses?
1. Aqua
2. Fire
3. Earth
4. Air
5. Light
6. Dark
7. Chaos

### How do I join a House?
1. Mint at least one DragN'Puff
2. Go to the Choose frame on Farcaster
3. Select your House
4. Your NFT pledges allegiance to that House

### Can I change Houses?
Yes! You can change your House choice at any time using the Choose frame.

### How is the leaderboard calculated?
Houses earn points from:
- Minting new NFTs (+10 points)
- Choosing a House (+5 points)
- Breathing Fire on opposing Houses (+15 points)
- Farcaster engagement (varies)

## Technical Questions

### What blockchain is this on?
Base Mainnet (ChainId: 8453), an Optimistic Rollup built on Ethereum.

### Do I need expensive gas for transactions?
No! Base has significantly lower gas fees than Ethereum (typically <$1 per transaction).

### How do I verify ownership of an NFT?
Use the Basescan Block Explorer:
1. Go to https://basescan.org
2. Search for the DragNPuff contract: 0x5eCbc3931C78169cbF682C9b15602EB8f9A42387
3. View token holdings for your address

### Can I trade DragN'Puff NFTs?
Yes! Trade on secondary markets like OpenSea, Blur, or others that support Base.

## Rewards & Scoring

### How do I earn points for my House?
- Mint NFTs
- Choose your House
- Use the "Breathe Fire" cast action on opposing House members
- Engage on Farcaster (likes, replies, etc.)

### What happens to top-performing Houses?
Winners receive recognition on the leaderboard and may unlock special rewards or new Houses.

### Are there prizes?
Prize structure is announced through Farcaster updates and the website.

## Security

### Is it safe to mint?
Yes! The contracts were audited and follow best practices. Always verify you're on https://dragnpuff.xyz

### What happens to my private key?
Your private key stays in your wallet at all times. Connect using standard wallet integrations (MetaMask, Rainbow Kit, etc.).

### How can I protect my NFTs?
- Store NFTs in a hardware wallet for large collections
- Never share your private key
- Use secure password managers
- Enable 2FA on email accounts
